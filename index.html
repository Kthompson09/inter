The Making of Inter

Enter Inter , the perfect open-source sans-serif typeface for computer user interfaces. Created By Rasmus Andersson back in 2017. Andersson started this font after noticing the “pitfalls” of the readability of the popular font Roboto. The man had text heavy Uis and typefaces in mind so he wanted to make his own font designed solely for computer user interfaces and offer it for free so that's what he did. By painstakingly analyzing the shortcomings of Roboto and applying specific rules to help push the typeface readability and pouring hours of time into this project he created Inter.
